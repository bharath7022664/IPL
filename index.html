TATA IPL 2023


From 2022, Tata Group replaces Vivo, a Chinese mobile phone manufacturer, as the title sponsor of the Indian Premier League. The IPL’s affiliation with the Tata Group as title sponsors was addressed at the league’s Governing Council meeting in January. Tata Group took over as the IPL 2022 sponsor till the end of the 2023 edition.
